Financial Ratios

We compute EBITDA margin as:

\[\text{EBITDA Margin} = \frac{\text{EBITDA}}{\text{Revenue}} \times 100\%\]

Weighted Average Cost of Capital (WACC):

\[\text{WACC} = \frac{E}{V}\cdot R_e + \frac{D}{V}\cdot R_d \cdot (1 - T_c)\]
